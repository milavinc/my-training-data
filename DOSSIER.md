### Recovery Protocol

**Recovery Triggers (Auto-Deload):**
- HRV ↓ > 20% → reduce intensity and evaluate recovery need
- RHR ↑ ≥ 5 bpm → reduce load, monitor closely
- Feel ≥ 4 → deload trigger
- Two+ triggers → switch to recovery-focused day or rest

**Feel Scale:**

| Score | Meaning |
|-------|---------|
| 1 | Excellent (fully recovered) |
| 2 | Good (normal fatigue) |
| 3 | Moderate (manageable tiredness) |
| 4 | Fatigued (reduced readiness, deload trigger) |
| 5 | Exhausted (complete rest required) |
